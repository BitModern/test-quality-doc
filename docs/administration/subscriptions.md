# Subscriptions

Manage your TestQuality subscription from this page. Two plan types are available: **Commercial Plan** and **GitHub Marketplace Plan**.

## Plans

TestQuality offers three paid tiers:

| Tier | Users included |
|---|---|
| Team | 10 users included |
| Business | 20 users included |
| Enterprise | 30 users included, custom options |

New accounts start with a 14-day free trial. After the trial, the account moves to a freemium plan and paid features are locked until a subscription is activated.

For full feature details and current pricing see the [TestQuality pricing page](https://www.testquality.com/#pricing).

## Commercial plan

1. Select your **Tier** from the dropdown
2. Select **Monthly** or **Annual** billing
3. Enter a **Coupon** code if you have one and click **Apply**
4. Review **User Seats** and **Price**
5. Enter your **Card holder name** and **Card information**
6. Click **Subscribe**

Your subscription renews automatically on the chosen billing cycle. If a payment cannot be processed, access to your site will be limited until the subscription is reactivated.

## GitHub Marketplace plan

If you purchased a subscription via GitHub Marketplace, click **GitHub Marketplace Plan** to apply your purchase.

## Auto-upgrade

When enabled, TestQuality automatically increases your seat count as you invite new users, removing the need to manually adjust your subscription each time.
